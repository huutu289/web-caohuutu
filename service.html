FACILITIES & SERVICE

VAIA RESTAURANT

A La Carte breakfast is served daily from 06:30 to 10:00 am, offering a wide selection of Western, Vietnamese or Hoi An local specialties with coffee and tea etc.

-----More Information-----

THE TERRACE CAFÉ

The Little Terrace Café is located in the lobby area. Breakfast is served here between the hours of 06:00 am and 10:00 am, free tea and coffee is available to guests throughout the day. During the daily Happy Hour between 04:00 – 06:00 pm one (1) free Special Cocktail of the Day per guest with snack is offered to all guests.

-----More Information-----

LAUNDRY SERVICES

Please, let us take care of all your laundry requirement. Your clothes are washed by hand and charged per kilo with a very reasonable cost. Detail of price is provided in the Room Directory.

-----More Information-----

TOUR DESK

Our Tour Desk is located at the hotel’s lobby offers a wide range of travel and transfer services to ensure that you have the best staying with greatest time in Hoi An. Please, make your booking at the Tour Desk or find more details in “Best recommendation for Best Experiences” brochure which is available in all room.
